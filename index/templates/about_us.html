!! BHARAT MATA KI JAY !!


Ashish Bhardwaj is a firebrand nationalist leader by heart and a devoted social activist by work and a journalist by profession.

Ashish Bhardwaj was born to Smt. Rima Devi and Shri Panini Upadhyay, in a lower-middle-class Brahmin family in the year 1995 in Ankraha(A tola of Chetma Village) of Patan block palamu district Jharkhand.

During his childish age, he lived in his parental village in a big joint family and his first teacher was his mother from where he got values and basic educations. Being the son of a mother who is not so much educated but believes in educating her children, so she along with his father shifted to Daltonganj(Nearby city to the village) for better education of her first child.


The early ages in this new city of this family were very difficult but somehow his father managed to enroll him in a famous school of that time in the city that is Gyan Niketan where he has completed his primary education. He was a bright student from his school days and took a lot of interest in historical subjects.

Further, he moved to Ranchi the capital of Jharkhand and completed his secondary and degree education from St. Xavier's College Ranchi. During his college days, he took active participation in co-curricular activities and there is the thought of bringing a change for the betterment of society came up in his mind. Further, he completed his higher education from NPU Daltonganj.

From childhood, he was a courageous boy full of self-confidence and moral values carrying the thoughts of legend Bhagat Singh Ji.He belives in the thought of "जननी जन्मभूमिश्च स्वर्गादपि गरीयसी" and "वीर भोग्या वसुंधरा". Apart from Bhagat Singh Ji, he is a blind disciple of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, Chandra Shekhar Azad and Subhas Chandra Bose.


He was groomed in a patriotic and nationalistic environment of his big joint family. Out of pumping nationalistic feelings, he went on to learn more about national heroes and legacy and eventually was highly touched by them as he is very close to his mother so he got values and discipline from there he has hired the qualities of a prolific leader as much valor, bravery, and perseverance for his goals from his family. The boon of nationalism was inside his heart from his childhood age and always ready to do anything and die for "Bharat Mata". There are uncountable stories of him that show his dedication and devotion towards this holy land India.

Since he was elder among three children of his parents and the condition of his family was not so good so he involved himself in various works to earn during his studies. He moved to many cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Varanasi, and many more to earn for his family but he didn't get satisfaction anywhere because he was interested in serving the nation. That fire of change didn't let him sleep.

As goes the saying destination is decided in the cradles proving the saying Ashish Bhardwaj started showing his impetus social dedication and love for the society from the very early days. He was highly inspired by the ideas of Shyama prasad Mukherjee, Pt. Din Dayal Upadhyay, and Atal Bihari Vajpayee and participated in various dialogues and debates and he found himself standing against the anti-nationals and those every word that smells like this. In the year 2014, he joined the Bhartiya Janta Party as a party worker and at present he is the leader of BJYM (Youth wing of Bhartiy Janta Party) ,palamu and continues to serve the party inspired by the idea of nationalism and devotion towards the nation, "विहाय कामान् य: कर्वान्पुमांश्चरति निस्पृह:। निर्ममो निरहंकार स शांतिमधिगच्छति।।" by following this shloka of Bhagwat Geeta he wants to fully commit himself for "Matri Bhoomi, to his Janani" soon Ashish Bhardwaj got it that it is this diaspora of RSS which will provide him the platform to serve India and it's people in the way he wanted to serve. In the same year of 2014 he started to serve as the "swayam Sevak" of राष्ट्रीय स्वयंसेवक संघ .


Despite having diaspora all set to go on and engross himself into personal love and growth, he aborted all these worldly pleasures. As the waves are uncontrollable hence were the laments of poor and underprivileged, torched, crushed people of society were making him a deafening scream in his ears demanding him to fully engross himself for social welfare. Janani was lamenting for her son to pay back her debt.

In the end, he could not take it anymore and ended up all his professional career and fully got himself indulged completely for the society.

There goes a famous saying that great people are not born rather they are born out of the naturalistic demand of the soil of their birth. Their population could be thin, sometimes countable on fingers. But the shine of this legacy becomes exempt ray and goes on to motivate generations. Some are born for themselves and some are to serve others and Ashish Bhardwaj is complete of the second type and proving himself day by day with his works every time whenever state, district, or his people requires him he stands over there for their betterment and well being he proves it by his work by serving the whole society during this massive covid period of the first and second phase. When people were fearing to come out from their homes he was on the ground for the people by his every means and in every way, he was serving them, either by providing them dry food, packed cooked food, medicines, oxygen cylinders, or by transporting them he was there for the people to serve them day and night continuously this shows his dedication towards his motherland and his people.


While leaving in Daltonganj a small city of Jharkhand This comrade Ashish Bhardwaj is often and continuously indulged in social welfare and upliftment programs by his means. He is working for Preserving cultural ethnicity working hand in hand together for all-round rocketing upliftment of our society whatever is in his capability, he is paying his everything to his Bharat Mata by serving the people, serving the nation he is a new ray of hope new radiants of a sunshine kiss through his Nobel work he is cherishing the mother India.
